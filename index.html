Dr. Enlai Gao (高恩来) received his B.S. in Engineering Mechanics from China Agricultural University (2013) and Ph.D in Solid Mechanics from Tsinghua University (2018). He is now an associate professor at Wuhan University (2018). His research interests are in the theory and modeling of low-dimensional materials and their assemblies. The purpose is to understand the mechanical behaviors of materials at the multi-scale, and guide the design and fabrication of advanced materials.

For more details please visit his research and publication pages.
