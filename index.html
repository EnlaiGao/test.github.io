RESEARCH INTERESTS
Combining experimental measurements with multi-level modeling techniques, such as Finite Element Method (FEM), Phase Field, Molecular Dynamics Simulation and First Principle Calculation, understand the nano and micro-mechanics of materials at the atomic level, and guide the design and synthesis of advanced materials.
通过理论、实验与跨尺度模拟，研究低维结构宏观组装纤维、薄膜的载荷传递机制与微结构演化规律，发展多尺度力学理论模型，理解并设计制备高性能、多功能低维结构宏观聚集体。相关成果在JMPS、JAM、Carbon、Nature Communications、Science等期刊发表SCI论文20余篇。
